About Dataless

Dataless uses Short Message Service (SMS) technology to give you all your news, weather, updates, and any other information you may need. If you are in an area with limited to no data, no problem. Dataless piggy-backs off of your SMS plan to bring you up-to-date information.

Our Vision:

By developing an interface capable of relaying information from Android-based applications via text messages, you’ll never need a data plan again. Every day another million user’s power up their Android devices for the first time - we’re here to make sure those without data plans stay connected. Whether it be helping farmers in India gain insight on rain patterns they otherwise would be incapable of predicting, providing a fourth grader in Mozambique with educational math resources, or allowing a low-income college student in Atlanta to search the web, we’re here to impact lives.
