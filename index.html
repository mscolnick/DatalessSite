No Data? No Problem.

Our Mission:

We believe that information should work to benefit all of humanity: the more we connect, the better it gets.

Our Vision:

By developing an interface capable of relaying information from Android-based applications via Short Message Service (SMS), you’ll never need a data plan again. Every day another million users power up their Android devices for the first time; we’re here to make sure these people stay connected. Whether it be helping farmers in India gain insight on rain patterns they otherwise would be incapable of predicting, providing a fourth grader in Mozambique with educational math resources, or allowing a low-income college student in Atlanta to search the web, we’re here to impact lives.
